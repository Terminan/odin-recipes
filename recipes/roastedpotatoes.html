Roasted Potatoes


Description

These potatoes are simple, yet so delicious! Never underestimate the nutrition and satiety of roasted potatoes.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350 fahrenheit.
  2. Cut the potatoes into 1/2 inch cubes.
  3. Put the potatoes onto a sheet pan and toss with oil and salt.
  4. Roast them in the oven until fork tender. Enjoy!
