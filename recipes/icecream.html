Homemade Ice Cream


Description

You'll love this recipe for rich, creamy homemade ice cream. The vanilla flavor will surprise you with just how much you enjoy it.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • Milk to fill the rest of the drum
  • Ice and rock salt for the ice cream machine

Instructions

  1. Mix together the sugar, sweetened condensed milk, vanilla extract, and eggs, and pour them into the drum of your ice cream maker.
  2. Fill the drum the rest of the way to about 3/4 full with your milk.
  3. Put the drum into your ice cream machine and surround it with layers of ice and rock salt.
  4. Turn your ice cream machine on and top off the ice and salt as it churns until it stops. At this point, your ice cream is ready!
